Cereal

Cereal dishes are a popular and versatile food, often enjoyed for breakfast but also suitable for snacks or light meals throughout the day. They consist of grains like wheat, oats, corn, or rice, often served with milk, yogurt, or fruit. Cereal can be hot or cold, depending on the type and how it's prepared.

Chicken soup

Chicken soup is a comforting and nourishing dish enjoyed around the world, known for its simplicity, warmth, and health benefits. It typically consists of chicken, vegetables like carrots, celery, onions, and various seasonings simmered in a flavorful broth.

Salmon and rice

Salmon rice is a flavorful and nutritious dish that combines tender, savory salmon with the comforting simplicity of rice. It's a versatile meal that can be prepared in many ways, depending on the cooking style and personal preferences. This dish is popular in various cultures, particularly in Japanese, Korean, and Western cuisines.
